# Transcriber
> This is part of my recipe summarizer project; more content will be added once it’s complete.

I often want to summarize YouTube videos using different AI tools, but most of them either can’t work without a transcript or require a paid plan. I figured—why not just add a Whisper transcriber myself? So I decided to code it out and turn it into an AI-powered utility.

By comparing various ASR services on the market, I selected Cloudflare for this purpose. Here are the research findings of the ASR options available: [Comparing ASR Solutions](/projects/comparing-asr-solutions).

## What it can do

* Fetch existing YouTube transcripts (if available).
* Generate transcriptions from any YouTube, video, or audio link — including Discord attachments.
* Transcribe in parallel, achieving 2–6× faster speeds.
* Completely free to use.

The Sample: [How to win: Steve Cohen on markets and the Mets](https://youtu.be/l3JLUFgUpkQ?si=Z1el3hVH9Ut5BjL5) has no built in subtitle, and the bot transcribed a 25mins video within a minute.

##  Parallel transcribing
By dividing the audio into segments and transcribing them in parallel, the transcription process becomes significantly faster. In the example below, this approach reduced the total processing time by 55 seconds. Cloudflare supports up to 16 concurrent requests before returning errors.

However, splitting audio introduces some overhead, and excessive segmentation can reduce transcription coherence. To balance performance and accuracy, a minimum chunk length of 150 seconds is set.

## Free quota
10,000 neurons is ~4.5 hours. When testing the parallel function with a 1.5-hour video, I quickly ran out of quota after just a few runs. Still, that’s more than enough for personal use. Cloudflare costs about $0.00051 per minute, which translates to 32.7 hours per dollar, quite affordable for most use cases.

If you are willing to pay more, I think lemonfox.ai offers the best value for ASR services, see  [Comparing ASR Solutions](/projects/comparing-asr-solutions) for comparison.
